# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (GitHub, discussions, issues, PRs) and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
